What is INNOVISION ?

INNOVISION a National Level Technical Symposium event which aims to bring together students, professionals, and industry experts from different parts of the country to showcase their technical skills, knowledge, and innovations.

Exciting Prizes

INNOVISION, provides a platform for participants to showcase their skills. It offers a prize pool of 5 lakhs and exciting prizes to motivate participants and make the event more rewarding.

Schedule

Event is scheduled to take place on 23rd and 24th March. It is an annual event that brings together students, professionals, researchers, and industry experts to showcase their technical skills and innovations.

Opportunities

It is an excellent platform for participants to establish a good network with the people from different parts of the country providing an opportunity to collaborate, and learn from each other.
